The Wish Machine
A story about saying exactly what you mean

Mia was a curious girl who loved adventures.
One sunny day, she found a mysterious workshop
with a glowing sign: "Professor Pixel's Lab"

Inside, she found the most incredible thing —
The Wish Machine! It glowed and hummed.
A tiny sign said: "Warning: Be SPECIFIC!"
But Mia was too excited to read it carefully...

💭 "I wish to be tall!"

⚡ ZZZZAP! ⚡ Mia shot up like a rocket!
She grew and grew until — BONK! — her head
crashed right through the ceiling! 🏠💥
"That's NOT what I meant!" she cried.

💭 "I wish I could fly!"

⚡ ZZZZAP! ⚡ Mia didn't get wings...
She turned into an actual fly! 🪰
"I said FLY, not BE a fly!" she buzzed angrily.
The machine just blinked: WISH GRANTED ✓

💭 "I want a million bucks!"

⚡ ZZZZAP! ⚡ The machine rumbled...
A MILLION deer appeared! 🦌🦌🦌
(Bucks = male deer, get it?)
"No no no! I meant MONEY!" Mia wailed.

💡 Mia's Big Realization

The machine wasn't broken — it did EXACTLY what she said!
The problem was how she asked.

🎯 The Secret: Say exactly what you mean!
❌ "I want to be tall" → grows forever
✅ "I want to be 5 feet tall" → just right!

❌ "I want to fly" → becomes a fly
✅ "I want to soar through the air with wings" → perfect!

❌ "I want a million bucks" → deer everywhere
✅ "I want a million dollars in my piggy bank" → 💰

Finally, Mia smiled. She typed carefully:
"I wish to feel happy and grateful,
right here, right now, as myself."

✨ And the machine glowed warmly... ✨
WISH GRANTED ✓

Because that wish was already true. 💛

~ The End ~
